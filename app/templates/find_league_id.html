How to find your FPL Draft Lague ID?

Caveat: Private or incognito browsers may not be able to retrieve the desired data.
The links on this page are provided by the offical Fantasy Premier League website.

Step 1:

Login to FPL Draft in your browser and leave this tab open: https://draft.premierleague.com

Step 2:

Open this link in a new tab in the same browser as step 1: https://draft.premierleague.com/api/bootstrap-dynamic

Step 3:

If successful, some text will appear in your browser tab and you will see your email address, name, region etc. If you dont see these things, try step 1 and 2 again.

Step 4:

Within this text, find where it says "leagues". You can use Ctrl+f or scroll until you see it. The leagues you are in will be listed here. It should look like this:
"leagues":[{"id":4815162342,"name":"Best League Ever","scoring":"h"}] - your id and name values will be different.

Step 5:

The id value you see from Step 4 is your FPL Draft League ID. If you are in multiple leagues, choose the id from the league you want to get insights for.
